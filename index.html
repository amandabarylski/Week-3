Welcome to the International Restoration Society!

Who We Are

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to the restoration and preservation of historic sites throughout the continent of Terraris. Though our main office is in Faren, we have members from many nationalities and have opened small branches in several cities. All revenue generated from donations and our campaigns goes to our projects and those working hard to complete them.

What We Do

  • Restore and translate historical documents
  • Restore ancient artwork according to records
  • Repair and renovate historic buildings
  • Restore and return historic items to their place of origin
  • Work with university and government teams to discover and excavate sites of interest
