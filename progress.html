Current Progress

Currently, we are working on stabilizing the Grand Ampitheatre in Kyrr to allow for visitors while maintaining the historic architecture; transcribing and rebinding the books that suffered water damage when Xanshir Library in ElErien flooded earlier this year; repairing the stained glass in Matheneas Cathedral in Trian; and excavating an unknown settlement buried in sand near Aali Oasis in collaboration with Amiira Royal University. See the table below for a summary of estimated costs, duration, and progress towards these projects. If one of them captures your interest and you are able to help support it, visit our donation page!

Project Cost Duration Progress
Grand Ampitheatre $1.6 mil 5 years 30%
Xanshir Library $600 K 10 months 80%
Matheneas Cathedral $50 K 6 months 75%
